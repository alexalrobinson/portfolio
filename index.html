BREADDIT

A Reddit-esque mobile-friendly site that gives users the ability to create new subreddits, create new posts and comment on others posts. Users have "Crumbs" instead of "Karma" that dictate how popular a user's posts and comments have been in the past. The user is able to upvote or downvote a post or comment just once, and it counts towards the creator's "Crumbs".

STUDY BREAK

Designed with the original purpose of Facebook in mind, Study Break was created with the idea of bringing students together. Each new user is automatically connected with the classmates in their grad year and major. Users are able to make new groups, post in them, view their feed and message other users. Happy to give a live demo.
